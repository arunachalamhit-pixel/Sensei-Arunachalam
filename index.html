Who I Am

About Sensei
Arunachalam

I am Arunachalam Vijayakumar, a 5th Dan Black Belt in Shito-Ryu Karate and a proud national karate athlete from Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. My journey began on competition floors across the state and has evolved into a lifelong mission to teach, inspire, and build champions.

From 2007 to 2017, I competed at the highest levels — earning medals at the Tamil Nadu State Championship, the National Championship, and several international invitationals. That decade of competitive fire shapes every class I teach today.

As Senior Instructor at Apex Martial Arts Academy, I have guided students from their first stance to black belt, and trained national-level athletes for the highest stages of competition.

🏆 State Champion 🥇 National Medalist 🌐 International Athlete ⚖️ WKF Kumite Judge 🎓 Certified Instructor 🏫 10+ Schools

"Karate begins and ends with respect. My goal is not only to produce skilled fighters, but to shape strong minds and strong character — one kata at a time."
